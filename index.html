Work Experience

My work experience includes:

  1. From February to March of 2020 I worked as a reporter for theSunDaily newspaper in Malaysia.
  2. From September to December 2019 I worked for IBC ltd and helped with transferring data as they were moving all their databases to SAP.

    IBC ltd are a Building Energy Management Systems company which provide equipment and systems necessary for making buildings more energy efficient. This job was useful to me as I learned what databases are used for in the real world and how they work, and also gave me a greater interest in computer science as I saw there were some very repetitive parts to my job (e.g copying and pasting many rows of data into a different sheet and ensuring it all adheres to a new format) which could be easily automated if I just had enough programming knowledge which would have saved both the company and I a lot of time and effort spent copying data which could have been better spent on other things.

  3. From June - July 2018 I interned at theSunDaily as a reporter writing about crime and politics.
